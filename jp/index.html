What could go wrong and how we reduce risk

All investing has risk. Gold and precious metals can fall fast, especially after sharp rallies. Currency moves can also affect returns. We reduce risk with size limits, staged entries, and clear exit triggers. This does not remove losses, but it can reduce large mistakes.

High impact / low frequency

Policy shock. Action: cut new entries and hold cash buffer.

High impact / higher frequency

Trend reversal. Action: trim positions when downside level breaks.

Lower impact / high frequency

Short-term volatility. Action: use tranche timing and patience rule.

Operational risk

Execution delay. Action: predefined broker and reporting checks.

Investor implication: risk is not ignored; it is planned with simple, visible rules.
